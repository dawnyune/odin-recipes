Pesto Chicken Penne Casserole

Description

Creamy, flavorful, and so easy to fix. This meal-in-one will wow family and friends. Makes a lot, so freeze some, or halve the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Bread Crumbs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Penne Pasta
  • Chicken
  • Shredded Italian Cheese Blend
  • Baby Spinach
  • Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Prehea an oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a baking dish. Combine bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, and olive oil in a bowl; set aside
  2. Fill large pot with salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Once boiling, stir in the penne, and return to a boil. Cook 11 minutes and drain colander set in sink.
  3. Meanwhile combine the chicken in a bowl with Italian cheese blend, spinach, tomatoes, alfredo sauce, pesto sauce, and milk.
  4. Stir in pasta once done, and scoop into prepared baking dish. Top with bread crumb mixture.
  5. Bake until bubbly and golden brown on top, 40 to 45 minutes.
